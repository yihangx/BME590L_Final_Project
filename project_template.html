My Project: Effect of Image Quality on Classification Accuracy

Mengxuan Cui     Yihang Xin

mengxuan.cui@duke.edu          yx141@duke.edu

Paper PDF

 

Image distortion is always a practical challenge in imaging system. So, it is important to characterize the effect of image quality on classification accuracy in convolutional neural network. Many types of image quality distortions in real world have effects on classifications accuracy and we are especially interested in the effect of blur, noise, pixel size, color field and contrast. We simulated image with low resolution, gaussian blur, gaussian noise, different color filters, and contrast filters. We found out that low image resolution, gaussian blur and gaussian noise would decrease classification accuracy. In some situations, using other color filters and contrast filters will increase classification accuracy.
